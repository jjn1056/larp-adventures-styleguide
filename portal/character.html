Biography

"Honor above all. Steel when honor fails."

Sir Aldric Stormwind was born in the northern reaches of Osterra, son of a blacksmith and a healer. When raiders attacked his village during the Winter of Ash, young Aldric took up his father's hammer to defend his home.

Impressed by his bravery, a passing knight of the Northern Alliance took Aldric as his squire. After ten years of training and service, Aldric was granted knighthood and given the name "Stormwind" for his prowess in battle during the Tempest Campaign.

Now Sir Aldric serves as a champion of the Northern Alliance, defending the realm against threats both mundane and supernatural. His goals are to protect the innocent, bring honor to his family name, and one day discover what truly happened to his parents after the raid.
