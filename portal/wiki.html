The Northern Alliance

The Northern Alliance
Type Major Faction
Founded Year 847 (in-game)
Leader High King Aldric III
Capital Winterhold
Territory Northern Reaches
Members ~45 active players
Allies The Free Cities
Rivals The Shadow Court

The Northern Alliance is one of the major player factions in LARP Adventures, known for their honorable warriors, strong defensive tactics, and loyalty to their allies. Based in the frozen northern territories, they have stood as a bulwark against the darkness for centuries.

This article contains spoilers

This article may contain plot details from recent events. Read at your own discretion.

History

The Northern Alliance was founded in the year 847 following the Great Sundering, when the northern clans united under a single banner to face the growing threat of the Frost Giants. What began as a temporary military alliance evolved into a permanent faction of kingdoms, bound by blood oaths and ancient traditions.

The Founding

The Alliance was forged at the Council of Winterhold, where seven clan chieftains swore the Oath of the Northern Star. This oath, still recited by new members today, binds all Alliance members to defend each other's lands and honor.

Recent Events

During the Darkfall Campaign (Season 12), the Northern Alliance played a crucial role in defending the Mountain Pass against the Shadow Court's invasion. Their sacrifice at the Battle of Frozen Tears is commemorated annually at the Midwinter Festival.

Organization

The Alliance operates as a confederation of semi-autonomous holds, each governed by a Jarl. The High King serves as supreme commander in times of war and mediator in times of peace.

Leadership Structure

  • High King - Elected by the Council of Jarls, serves for life
  • Council of Jarls - Seven ruling lords, one from each founding clan
  • Thanes - Noble warriors who serve the Jarls
  • Huscarls - Elite personal guards of the nobility
  • Freemen - Common soldiers and citizens

Military Forces

The Alliance fields several specialized military units:

  • The Shield Wall - Heavy infantry famous for their defensive formations
  • Winter Wolves - Scout and ranger company
  • Storm Riders - Cavalry shock troops
  • The Runewardens - Battle mages specializing in protective magic

Culture & Traditions

Northern culture emphasizes honor, martial prowess, and loyalty to one's sworn brothers and sisters. Key traditions include:

Oath-Taking

All members of the Alliance must swear the Oath of the Northern Star upon joining. Breaking this oath is considered one of the gravest offenses and can result in exile or worse.

Major Festivals

  • Midwinter Festival - Celebrates the longest night and honors fallen heroes
  • Thawmeet - Spring festival marking the end of winter isolation
  • The Proving - Annual tournament where warriors compete for glory

How to Join

Players interested in joining the Northern Alliance should:

  1. Create a character with a background compatible with northern culture
  2. Contact a current member or the faction leader in-game
  3. Complete the in-game initiation quest "The Trial of Frost"
  4. Attend at least one event to be formally inducted
OOC Note: The Northern Alliance is always welcoming new members! Contact @HighKingAldric on Discord for more information.
