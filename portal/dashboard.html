Updated 2 hours ago by Rules Committee View Full Rules
Section 4.2: Grappling Mechanics

Grappling requires a successful hit call followed by "GRAPPLE" within 2 seconds.

Grappling requires a successful hit call followed by "GRAPPLE" within 3 seconds. Target may call "RESIST" if they have the appropriate skill.

Section 5.1: Shield Bash

Shield bash may be performed once every 10 seconds.

Shield bash may be performed once every 15 seconds. Cooldown begins after the bash lands or is blocked.

Section 5.3: Defensive Stance (New)

Players with the Defender path may call "DEFENSIVE STANCE" to reduce incoming damage by 1 for 30 seconds. May be used once per encounter.
