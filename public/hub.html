Character Creation

Build your hero and choose your path.

The Four Paths

Warrior's Path

Masters of combat and defense. Warriors protect their allies and lead the charge.

Rogue's Path

Experts in stealth, subterfuge, and precision strikes. Rogues excel at reconnaissance and assassination.

Wizard's Path

Wielders of arcane power. Wizards command the elements and bend reality to their will.

Healer's Path

Servants of life and restoration. Healers mend wounds and shield their allies from harm.

Getting Started
  1. Choose a Path
    Pick the path that fits your playstyle.
  2. Create Your Background
    Where does your character come from? What do they want?
  3. Select Starting Skills
    New characters start with 10 XP to spend on skills.
  4. Prepare Your Costume
    Dress the part! We have guides and loaner items available.
