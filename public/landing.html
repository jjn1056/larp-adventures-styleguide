What is LARP?

Live Action Roleplaying (LARP) is collaborative storytelling where you become your character.

Combat & Adventure

Engage in safe, foam-weapon combat. Lead raids against enemy forces. Defend your allies in the heat of battle.

Roleplay & Story

Create a unique character with their own goals and backstory. Navigate politics, solve mysteries, and forge relationships.

Community

Join a welcoming community of adventurers. Make friends, share stories, and create memories that last a lifetime.

Safety First

LARP Adventures is committed to creating a safe, inclusive environment for all players. Our safety policies include:

  • All weapons are foam-padded and safety-checked before each event
  • Clear consent mechanics for roleplay interactions
  • Trained safety marshals at all events
  • Code of conduct with zero tolerance for harassment
  • Accessibility accommodations available upon request
