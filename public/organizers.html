Staff Roles

At each event, you'll encounter staff members in various roles:

Game Masters (GMs)

GMs run the story and encounters. They're identifiable by their orange headbands when out of character.

Contact them for: Plot questions, story hooks, NPC interactions

Safety Marshals

Marshals ensure combat safety and handle any safety concerns. They wear red armbands.

Contact them for: Weapon checks, safety concerns, medical issues

Check-In Staff

Handle registration, payments, and new player orientation at the start of events.

Contact them for: Registration issues, loaner gear, cabin assignments

New Player Mentors

Experienced players who volunteer to help newcomers during their first event.

Contact them for: Rules help, character advice, finding your way around
