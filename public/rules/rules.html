Cleaned from the Discord #in-game-rules channel export. Decorative images and Discord UI markup were removed; linked rule resources are preserved.

Character Creation

The Character Creation process at Larp Adventures is flexible, allowing for all manner of concepts to come about.

You have up until you reach Tier 3, which is 7 attended events, to solidify your main class. At that point, your spent skill points become locked in and you cannot change your primary class.

A handy How-To for Creation

The Warrior's Path

A Warrior is a skilled and formidable force on the battlefield. Strength and Confidence combine to bring forth the fiercest among us.

Skills & Abilities

The Warrior's Path skills and abilities can be found in Warrior Path Skills & Abilities.

The Rogue's Path

Rogues are cunning, and can often strike from the shadows with unseen advance. If you seek a bit of the fleet-footed work that may lend itself well to operating in the background, look no further.

Skills & Abilities

The Rogue's Path skills and abilities can be found in Rogue Path Skills & Abilities.

The Healer's Path

While Healers keep their teams up and fighting, their skill and intensity should not be undersold. Either mundane or divine, Healer's are integral to any party and are some of our brightest and most observant minds.

Skills & Abilities

The Healer's Path skills and abilities can be found in Healer Path Skills & Abilities.

The Mage's Path

Those who seek the path of the Mage often have a flair for the dramatics. Combinations of intellect, utility, and raw power in any measure can be found within the guild.

Skills & Abilities

The Mage's Path skills and abilities can be found in Mage Path Skills & Abilities.

Professions

When you reach Tier 3, you are able to begin the path down a Profession. We currently have three available to every class. Professions cannot replace a main class, but can supplement for flavor and character development.

Merchants

Shopkeeps of Osterra that choose to buy into the Merchant's Guild are afforded certain perks and protections. There is an application process, however, and you should seek out Trez Arrigo to apply.

Note: You may have a SHOP without being a part of the Merchant's Guild Game.

Bardic Arcana

The Bardic Profession offers magic in small quantities to those with creative prospects.

Bardic skills and abilities can be found in Bardic Arcana Skills & Abilities.

Artificers

Industrious and inventive Osterrans may find benefit in tinkering and learning how to craft or improve upon things already in play

Artificer skills and abilities can be found in Artificer Skills & Abilities.

Scholar

Those more academically inclined may find the scholarly pursuits more to their taste. Lore builders take heed, there is much to learn still of Osterra and her workings.

Scholar skills and abilities can be found in Scholar Skills & Abilities.
