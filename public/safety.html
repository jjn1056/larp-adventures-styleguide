Safety at Events

Your safety is always our top priority. Here's how we keep everyone safe during events:

Safety Marshals

Our trained safety marshals wear red armbands and are available throughout every event. Find them if you need help with any safety concern.

The "HOLD" Call

Anyone can call "HOLD" to immediately pause all game activity for a real-world safety issue. When you hear "HOLD", stop moving and wait for instructions.

First Aid Stations

Every event has clearly marked first aid stations with trained responders. Locations are announced at check-in and marked on site maps.

Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness any behavior that makes you uncomfortable, we encourage you to report it. You have multiple options:

In-Person at Events

Find any staff member or safety marshal (red armband). They can assist you immediately and connect you with the appropriate resources.

Email Report

Send a detailed report to safety@larpadventures.com. All reports are reviewed by our safety team within 48 hours.

Anonymous Reporting

Use our anonymous reporting form in the Player Portal if you prefer not to identify yourself. We take all reports seriously.

Emergency Line

For urgent safety matters during events, call our event hotline. The number is provided at check-in for each event.
