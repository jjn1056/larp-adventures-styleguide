What Happened

The Winter Council convened as darkness spread from the fallen stronghold of Ironhold. Alliances were tested, secrets revealed, and the fate of the Eastern Realms hung in the balance.

Friday Evening

The Refugees Arrive

Dwarven survivors from Ironhold stumbled into camp, bearing news of the fortress's fall. Players organized emergency shelter and heard firsthand accounts of the horrors beneath the mountain.

Saturday Morning

The Council Convenes

Faction leaders gathered to debate the response. The Northern Alliance called for immediate military action, while the Arcane Covenant urged caution and investigation. Tensions ran high as accusations flew.

Saturday Afternoon

The Shadow's Reach

Strange creatures emerged from the forest—scouts of whatever consumed Ironhold. A desperate battle ensued, with players uniting across faction lines to defend the camp.

Saturday Night

Secrets of the Hammer

A hidden message from King Thorgrim was decoded, revealing the location of the Hammer of Making—and that the darkness could potentially be contained with it. A quest party was assembled.

Sunday Morning

The Pact

Against all odds, the factions agreed to a temporary alliance. The Treaty of Silver Woods was signed, committing all signatories to aid in the recovery of the Hammer. The hunt begins at Rites of Spring.
