Rules Update

Combat Rules Update: Version 3.2

January 15, 2026 | Rules Committee

We've published an update to the combat rules based on community feedback. Key changes include:

  • Shield Bash: Cooldown reduced from 30 seconds to 15 seconds
  • Backstab: Now requires 3 seconds of being undetected (was 5 seconds)
  • Ranged Weapons: Maximum bow draw weight increased to 25 lbs (was 20 lbs)
  • Clarification: Added language about "partial blocks" and grazing hits

These changes are effective immediately. Please review the updated rulebook before the next event.
