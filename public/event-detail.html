About This Event

The veil between worlds grows thin as winter yields to spring. Ancient rituals must be performed to ensure the harvest, but dark forces seek to corrupt the ceremony. Heroes from across Osterra must unite to protect the sacred groves and ensure that spring arrives on schedule.

This event will feature:

  • A major storyline advancing the ongoing Osterra narrative
  • Multiple faction-specific plot threads
  • New player orientation and loaner gear
  • Night battles and ritual encounters
  • Opportunities for all character paths

What to Bring

  • Sleeping bag and pillow (bunks provided)
  • Costume/garb appropriate for a fantasy medieval setting
  • Toiletries and any medications
  • Flashlight or headlamp (red filter preferred)
  • Weather-appropriate outdoor clothing
  • Your own LARP-safe weapons (optional - loaners available)

Meals

All meals (Friday dinner through Sunday lunch) are included in weekend packages. Please note any dietary restrictions during registration.

Accessibility

We strive to make our events accessible to all. The main buildings are wheelchair accessible. Please contact us before registering if you have specific accessibility needs so we can work with you.
