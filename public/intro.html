The LARP Adventures Experience

What makes us different

Immersive Storytelling

Step into the world of Osterra, a hub-world where portals connect to countless realms. The EverWar between light and darkness rages across the multiverse, and you've been called to make a difference.

"Your actions become tales for the bards to sing."

Safe & Inclusive

We welcome adventurers of all backgrounds and experience levels. Our safety-first approach includes weapon checks, consent mechanics, trained marshals, and a zero-tolerance policy for harassment.

"Everyone deserves to be a hero."

Accessible & Affordable

No experience necessary. We provide loaner weapons, basic tabards, and new player orientation at every event. Day adventures start at just $10, with pay-what-you-can options available.

"All you need is a sense of adventure."
