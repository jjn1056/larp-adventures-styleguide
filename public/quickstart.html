Quick Start Guide

The shortest path from first-event prep to the current rules.

First Event Minimum

Bring a character name, basic garb, and an idea of which path interests you. The rest can come with time, guild help, and event play.

Start Here

Character Concept
  • Use an original character, not a canon media character.
  • Stay within medieval fantasy aesthetics.
  • Decide whether your character is native to Osterra or came through a portal.
Creation How-To
Kit and Garb
  • Comfort and safe movement matter first.
  • A simple tunic, belt, pants, and boots is enough to begin.
  • Modern sci-fi aesthetics and firearms are not viable in the setting.
Vendor Ideas

Skill Points

At your first event, your character is Tier 0. Day events award 1 skill point, campout events award 2, and festival events award 3. You may take up to 3 Tier 0 skills at your first event, and up to 3 at each later event until you have acquired all Tier 0 skills for your primary class.
