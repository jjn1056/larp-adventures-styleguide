The Healer’s Path

Skills, spells, and abilities for Healers
LARP Adventures
Tier 0 Abilities

Read/Write Arcana: Player can decipher magic runes and read magic. This skill is foundational and a prerequisite for all other spells. Allows for the casting of all spells at a walk.
Physical Representation: Rune cipher in spellbook (Elder Futhark is the standard runic system used for magic at LARP Adventures)
Limitations: Study, research, and quests may be needed to unlock some magics. Does not restrict players from flavoring their healer abilities to be Non-Magical.

Light/Darkness: (Spell) Create a light equal to 10 feet per tier or create an area of magical Darkness equal to 10 feet per tier.
Physical representation: 10 words in spellbook, a light producing implement or Black cloth to represent Darkness
Limitations: Can only have one in play at a time

First Aid: Restores use of a wounded limb, but does not heal it. Further damage to a bandaged limb will cause a character a mortal wound. This skill may also be used to “stabilize” another player, bringing them from “Dead” (Can not engage in play by any means) to “Mortalled” (can call out desperately for aid, may crawl away from combat or to original battle lines) or Mortalled to “Stable” (can move slowly, cannot use abilities or engage in combat). A player needs further healing to be Combat Ready.
Physical Representation: Bandage or wrap
Limitations: This ability is only limited by the number of bandages carried by the player.

Sense Blessed/Cursed: (Spell) 10 word spell in spellbook. Can detect if object Blessed/Cursed, but not any details or effects. The invocation line should indicate which ailment is being searched for, as each casting can only detect one type of ailment.
Limitations: Single person or object, may be used 3x per day +1 per Healer Tier.

Basic Weapon Proficiency: Players learn the basic combat and safety rules and the use of hand weapons and throwables. The Healer can now utilize single handed weapons, staves, LARP safe throwables, and two handed bludgeoning weapons such as hammers, maces.

Armor Proficiency: Players learn about the armor that they wear and about its effective and safe use in play. A healer’s Maximum starting Armor Point total is 2.

Shield Control: Players learn the art of Shield Control and may use shields in combat.

Tier 1 Abilities

Heal Minor: (Spell) Heals 1 point of damage to a limb.
Physical Representation: 15 word spell in spellbook. Holy Symbol or focus item (like larp medkit or LARP safe doctor’s tools)
Limitations: May be used 3x per day +1 per Healer Tier, may not be used to heal mortal wounds, only able to be cast on a single character per use.
Prerequisite: First Aid

Sanctify/Defile Location: (Spell) Area marked cannot be exited or entered by beings of a predetermined alignment or with a specified and simple intent (harm the caster, infiltrate the fort, steal the object, etc.). Combat can occur through an area surrounded by a Sanctify/Defile location spell, and beings of the predetermined alignment will take double damage from non-ranged attacks from those inside the Location.
Physical Representation: 25 word spell in spellbook. White ribbon around location, or object, and a written message denoting tier and caster's name
Limitations: 1x per Tier per day, 10’ radius per tier, a healer may have one sanctified location active per tier.

Detect Ailment: (Spell) The Player can detect disease or poison, identify its potential effects on characters, items, food/beverages, and other creatures of the realm.  In some cases, The Healer may also gain knowledge of the source. If a cure to the disease/poison is known in the world, the caster also gains knowledge of the cure.
Physical Representation: 25 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: 3x per day +1 per Healer Tier. Only able to be cast on a single character or item.

Divine Blessed/Cursed: (Spell)  Once detected with Sense Blessed/Cursed, Divine Blessed/Cursed will give the details and effects of said blessed/cursed object, person, or location. The Healer may also gain knowledge of the source as well as any known ways to cure or alleviate the effect outside of Remove Blessing/Curse.
Physical Representation: 25 Word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: 3x per day +1 per Healer Tier. Only able to be cast on a single character or item.

Console: (Spell) Players influenced by the “fear” status effect will flee from the enemy that has caused the effect for 5 seconds or will wither to the ground, unable to fight. Console negates this effect for a single target player after the effect has taken hold, and prevents that player from receiving the fear effect again for 1 hour.
Physical Representation: 25 Word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: 3x per day +1 per Healer Tier.

Minor Blessing: The Healer now has access to the Tome of Blessings & Curses held by the Healer’s Guild. The Healer may choose 1 of the 3 Minor Blessings in play to place on a single item. When that item is held or possessed by another player, they are placed under the effect of the Blessings imparted. May be used 1 time per day. This skill may always be used to make a weapon Holy, allowing the weapon to deal holy damage with maximum casts of 1 per healer tier per day.
Physical Representation: An item, with a Blue ribbon/band around it with the name of the blessing attached. Effect of Blessing should be written and available in a card format for the player with the item that has received the effect. 25 words in spellbook and roleplay.
Limitations: Healer may only have one item Cursed or Blessed in play at a time. The maximum Tier of the Blessing is equal to the Healer Tier of the Caster.
Prerequisite: Read/Write Arcana
Tier 2 Abilities:

Heal Major: (Spell) Restores all Hit Points to all locations of a single player. Does not restore armor or any floating hit points/armor points, nor does it cure any ailments or physical conditions.
Physical Representation: 30 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 3x per day +1 per Healer Tier.
Prerequisite: Heal MInor

Ritual Casting: Player gains the ability to lead or join a ‘Ritual’. Ritual casting is used when the tier or power of a single caster would not be enough to achieve the desired effect. Each individual participating in the ritual may cast a single spell of their choice, and add the tier of the spell they cast to the effect of the effect being created, or expanding the area or number of items affected as appropriate. Each individual can only contribute one spell to each ritual. This ability can be used to achieve effects beyond normal spellcasting, as appropriate and approved by an organizer present.

Scrolls & Potions: The Healer gains the ability to produce scrolls and potions. Potions may be consumed by anyone, Scrolls may only be used if the user knows Read/Write Arcana.
To Create Potions or Scrolls, please refer to the full Scroll/Potion ruleset for limitations and mechanics.

Holy Armor: (Spell)  Absorbs the first 1 point of damage taken by the target, regardless of location. Once 1 point of damage is absorbed the spell fades, and the belt flag should be removed at first opportunity.
Physical Representation: 35 word spell in spellbook, red belt flag with one white stripe, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier. Holy Armor may not be stacked with other Holy Armors, or Arcane Armor.

Remove Curse/Blessing: (Spell) Can be used to remove curse/blessing on players, objects, and locations. Before breaking an unknown curse/blessing, the magic in question must be identified through the use of “Divine Blessed/Cursed.” Remove Blessing/Curse is successful only if the tier of the caster is equal to or higher than the tier of the Curse/Blessing being dispelled.
Physical Representation: 35 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier, single object, person, location (10’ sq)
Prerequisite: Divine Blessed/Curse

Minor Curse: The Healer, using the Tome of Blessings & Curses, may now choose 1 of the 3 Minor Curses in play to place on a single item. When that item is held or possessed by another player, they are placed under the effect of the Curse imparted. Can be used 1 time per day. This skill can always be used to make an item or weapon Unholy, allowing the weapon to deal unholy damage with maximum casts of 1 per healer tier per day
Physical Representation: An item, with a Blue ribbon/band around it with the name of the Curse attached. Effect of Curse should be written and available in a card format for the player with the item that has received the effect. 35 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: Healer may only have one item Cursed or Blessed in play at a time. The maximum Tier of the Curse is equal to the Healer Tier of the Caster.
Prerequisite: Minor Blessing

Cross-Training:  At Tier 2 you may train in one of the other Skill Paths. Cross Training skills are repeatable. This skill allows the player to explore Tier 0 and Tier 1 skills in other trees at the cost of 2 skill points and require all necessary prerequisites of the specific skill.
Warrior Training 1: The Player dedicates time to learning the craft of warriors. They can choose one tier 1 or lower ability from the Warriors Skill path.
Mage Training 1: The Player studies the craft of the magi of the realm. They can choose one tier 1 or lower ability from the Mage skill path.
Rogue Training 1: The Player studies and watches the rogues around them. They can choose one tier 1 or lower ability from the Rogue skill path.
Tier 3 Abilities:

Cure Ailment: (Spell)  Target poison of a lesser or equal tier to the caster or any disease is neutralized and further effects are negated, but any Hit Point Damage will still need to be healed.
Physical Representation: 50 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier above 4.
Prerequisite: Detect Disease/Poison

Heal Senses: (Spell) The player may heal/restore the senses of a target player. This spell will restore sight, hearing, and touch. It will also restore the voice of a character that has been silenced.
Physical Representation: 50 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier above 3.

Heal Group Minor: (Spell) The player may stabilize or heal 1 hit point from a group of players, up to their Healer tier.
Physical Representation: 45 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay, each player must be pointed to (from within 10”) or touched.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier above 3, may not be used to heal torso/mortal wounds, Target number of persons up to caster’s highest tier. See Heal (minor) for other mechanics/effects
Prerequisite: Heal Major

Self Sacrifice: (Spell) The player may take the place of a wounded or killed player. This spell may also be used to assume the affliction of another player, such as blindness, or silence, or take on the illness of another player, such as poison, or disease effects. If the target player was deceased or injured, that player is healed and restored to play, including armor but not any other form of protection. The caster assumes all effects of the injury or affliction.
Physical Representation: 30 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day, gaining an additional use every three tiers.

Warp Wood: (Combat Magic) The player has learned to channel the negative energy of nature into an offensive power that they can throw at enemies or objects. A target hit by Warp Wood/Decay will warp all wood and decay natural materials on a humanoid-sized target rendering them unusable. Warp Wood will affect items including shields, bows, arrows, crossbows, bolts, javelins, polearms. A single Warp Wood/Decay will not affect large items such as siege weapons, gates, ships, ect., but a game organizer may determine if, and how many Warp Wood/Decay spell attacks may be able to damage some larger than humanoid sized targets.
Damage: 0, Necrotic
Physical Representation: 30 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay. 6” Brown Spell Ball
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per every 2 Healer Tiers.

Compel Truth: (Spell) The player may ask another player, or NPC, a number of yes or no questions equal to their highest Healing Tier. The target player or NPC must answer the questions to the best of their abilities and may answer with a yes, no, or brief unambiguous statement.
Physical Representation: 50 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier above 3. Iron Will, Mind Shield, and other abilities or spells with similar mental effects will reduce or negate Compel Truth based on Tier of user. An individual with Iron Will or Mind shield halves the number of questions Compel truth can force them to answer. Additionally if their Mage (Mind Shield) or Warrior(Iron Will) Tier is higher than the caster’s Healer Tier, Compel Truth has no effect.

Inflict Minor Curses and Wounds: (Combat Magic) The Healer summons spiritual energy that can be thrown at target player or NPC that either does 1 point of holy/unholy damage or imparts 1 of the 3 available  minor curses from the Tome of Blessings & Curses.
Physical Representation: 30 word spell written in spellbook and roleplay. Minimum 3” black spellball, alternatively character themed coreless throwing weapons painted black may be used, such as darts, knives, and hammers. If a curse is being imparted, an invocation line including the curse name and effect should be added on to the spell, minimum an additional 5 words.
Limitations: All curses inflicted this way are removed if the affected player receives a Coup de Grâce style blow. May be used 1 time per day, with an additional use added for every healer tier above 3. Damage and curses are blocked by shields and armor.

Speak With Dead: (Spell) The player is able to contact and communicate with the dead and is able to ask 3 questions. The soul of the deceased must answer questions truthfully and to the best of their abilities.A creature is not aware of any questions or answers given when returned to life.
Physical Representation: 50 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier above 3.

Major Blessing: The Healer, using the Tome of Blessings & Curses, may now choose 1 of the 3 Major Blessings in play to place on a single item. When that item is held or possessed by another player, they are placed under the effect of the Blessing imparted. Physical Representation: An item, with a Blue ribbon/band around it with the name of the Blessing attached. Effect of Blessing should be written and available in a card format for the player with the item that has received the effect. 50 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: Healer may only have one item Cursed or Blessed in play at a time. The maximum Tier of the Blessing is equal to the Healer Tier of the Caster.
Prerequisite: Minor Curse
Tier 4 Abilities:

Holy Armor 2:  (Spell) Absorbs the first 2 points of damage taken by the target, regardless of location. Once 2 points of damage are absorbed the spell fades, and the belt flag should be removed.
Physical Representation: 75 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay. Red belt flag with two white stripes.
Limitations: May be used 2x per day +1 per Healer Tier above 4. Holy Armor 2 may not be stacked with other Holy Armors, or Arcane Armor.
Prerequisite: Holy Armor

Heal Group Major: (Spell) An amount of targets, equal to the healers tier, are healed completely from a mortally wounded state or less.
Physical Representation: 60 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay, each player must be pointed to (from within 10”) or touched.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier above
Prerequisite: Heal Group Minor

Augury: (Spell) When cast the player will receive an omen or message from an otherworldly entity about the results of a specific course of action that the players plan to take within the next 30 minutes. Other information can be provided at the organizer’s discretion
Physical Representation: 75 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day. Requires Game Official to be present.
Hold Person: (Combat Magic) Player summons spiritual energy in the form of a sphere that may be thrown at the target player or NPC. If a target is hit by the Hold Person spell they are frozen in place for 15 seconds per healer tier. May not be blocked by shields.
Physical Representation: 50 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay. 6” White Spell Ball
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per every 2 Healer Tiers.

Major Curse: The Healer, using the Tome of Blessings & Curses, may now choose 1 of the 3 Major Curses in play to place on a single item. When that item is held or possessed by another player, they are placed under the effect of the Curse imparted.
Prerequisite: Major Blessing
Physical Representation: An item, with a Blue ribbon/band around it with the name of the Blessing attached. Effect of Curse should be written and available in a card format for the player with the item that has received the effect. 75 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: Healer may only have one item Cursed or Blessed in play at a time. The maximum Tier of the Curse is equal to the Healer Tier of the Caster.

Imbue with Smite: (Combat Magic) The Healer has developed the ability to gather and focus their spiritual energy and faith onto a weapon to deliver a powerful attack. Once cast, the next attack that lands on an opponent anywhere (weapons and shields) by the imbued weapon does 1 additional damage, with additional damage added for every other Healer tier over 4. That damage counts as holy or unholy.
Physical Representation: 75 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May only be used once per encounter, up to a maximum of 3 times per adventure day, with an additional use added every other Healer Tier over 4. Does not stack with any other damage multipliers/buffs

Animate Dead: (Spell) The caster’s mastery over death has reached the point where a body may be reanimated from death to create a minion to serve them. The targeted creature is Reanimated as an Animated Dead. During Casting, the caster may select to animate the creature or player as either a Zombie (2 HP, can only walk) or a Skeleton (1 HP, moves freely ignores thrusts and arrows). Both attack with Necrotic damage. The most basic form of reanimation, these creatures are dependent on the orders of their master and can only follow the most basic of commands. In absence of commands from their master, they attack the nearest creature until destroyed.
Phys Rep: 75 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay. Skeleton Mask or Gray Headband, Zombie Mask or Black headband
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per every 2 Healer/Mage Tiers. Players may decline to be animated. A player may only have one Animated Dead in play per 2 Tiers
Prerequisite: Speak with Dead
Swift Heal: (Spell) Once this spell is written in the player's spell book, the player may half the casting time of basic Healing spells. The word length of such spells may be reduced by 50%, rounded up.
Physical Representation: 200 word reference in spellbook.
Limitations: Only applies to Heal Minor, Heal Major, and the group versions of those spells.
Prerequisite: Heal Group Minor

Cross-Training: At Tier 4 you may train in one of the other Skill Paths. The player must have the previous training level in the appropriate path to pursue the second stage of cross path training. Cross Training skills are repeatable.
Warrior Training 2: The Player dedicates time to learning the craft of warriors. They can choose one tier 2 or lower ability from the Healer skill path.
Mage Training 2: The Player studies the craft of the magi of the realm. They can choose one tier 2 or lower ability from the Mage skill path.
Rogue Training 2: The Player studies and watches the rogues around them. They can choose one tier 2 or lower ability from the Rogue skill path.
Tier 5 Abilities:

Resurrection: (Spell) Target is brought back to life from death. They are fully healed and can remember their death and the events leading up to it. Does restore armor and other items
Physical Representation: 100 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier above 5.
Prerequisite: Self Sacrifice

Specialist: Player may gain additional mana points equal to half their healer tier (rounded up) for use with the ‘Scrolls and Potions’ skill for the creation of potions.
Special: While using preferred Physical Representations with instructions written in their spellbook to convey their use, all spells that detect ailments, cure ailments, and heal senses casting times are halved.
Physical Representation: 100 word Reference in spellbook, and roleplay.
Prerequisite: Detect Ailment

Healer’s Bulwark: Once this spell is written in the player's spell book, The Healer is able to use their shield as their spell focus and it is imbued with magical resistance. The healer may now cast without referencing their spellbook. A good effort should be made to affix representations of the healer's spell on the back of their shield, and casting time remains the same, and players must recite invocation lines. The shield is now also able to block magical attacks up to the wielder’s tier and resist magical shield breaking effects up to the healer's tier.
Physical Representation: 200 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay. The caster must memorize their spells, and/or have them written on the back of the shield, to jog their memory.
Prerequisite: Shield Training

Inflict Major Curses and Wounds: (Combat Magic) The Healer summons spiritual energy that can be thrown at target player or NPC that either does 2 points of holy/unholy damage or imparts a major curse from the Tome of Blessings & Curses.
Physical Representation: 50 word spell written in spellbook and roleplay. Minimum 3” black spellball, alternatively character themed coreless throwing weapons painted black may be used, such as darts, knives, and hammers. If a curse is being imparted, an invocation line including the curse name and effect should be added on to the spell, minimum an additional 5 words.
Limitations: All curses inflicted this way are removed if the affected player receives a Coup de Grâce style blow. May be used 1 time per day, with an additional use added for every healer tier above 5. Damage and curses are blocked by shields and armor.

Create Undead Knight: (Spell) Continued study in the necromantic arts allows the player to increase the strength of their undead minions. The caster may now create their Skeleton or Zombie as a Knight, which triples their hit point pool and allows them to speak and follow more advanced commands. The Knight may now also use Mighty Blow a single time if they wield a two handed weapon.
Physical Representation: 100 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay. Gray headband with two black skulls or an even meaner skeleton mask.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per every 2 Healer Tiers. Players may decline to be animated. A player may only have one Undead Knight in play per 3 Tiers of experience.
Prerequisite: Animate Dead

Master of the Tome: The Healer now gains the ability to utilize Blessings/Curses that have been in The Tome of Blessings & Curses at any time, without having to adhere to the seasonal selection of the blessings and curses. Additionally, the player may now have up to two cursed/blessed items in play at a time. The healer may now also attempt to create Minor & Major Blessings and Curses by working with Organizers.
Prerequisite: Major Blessing & Major Curse
Physical Representation: 100 word reference in spell book

Tier 6 Abilities

Animate Group of Dead: (Spell) A number of targeted creatures up to your tier may now be Reanimated as Animated Dead. If cast on a player they are Animated for 10 minutes or until slain; after they may continue to role play as the undead minion of the casting player until death if they wish, or return to play as normal, with no memory of their death or what they did while animated. During Casting, may select to animate individual creatures or players as either a Zombie or a Skeleton. The most basic form of reanimation, these creatures are dependent on the orders of their master and can only follow the most basic of commands. In absence of commands from their master, they attack the nearest creature until destroyed.
Physical Representation 100 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay. See Animate Dead for specifics.
Limitations:  May be used 1x per day +1 per every 2 Healer/Mage Tiers. Players may decline to be animated.(See Animate Dead)
Special: Upon taking Animate Group of Dead, the limit of the number of Animated Dead under your control is increased to 1 Per Healer/Mage Tier
Prerequisite: Create Undead Knight

Restore Memory: (Spell) Restore Memory gives the caster the ability to bring back lost memories from the target’s previous lives, or memories lost, or shrouded by other magics. When a Healer casts Restore Memory the target player or NPC can recall what happened in their last life, and the circumstances surrounding and deaths during that day/event. In the case of memories lost, or shrouded by other magics an Organizer may be necessary in order to determine the Tier of the other caster, and nature of the memories to be recovered.
Physical Representation: 100 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay.
Limitations: If the caster of the magics clouding the memories to be restored is greater than the Tier of the Healer, then they can not be restored. Can only be used 1 time per day + 1x per healer tier after 6th.

Create Relic: (Spell) The player imbues an item with Holy/Unholy energy, Relic weapons deal +1 damage, all damage from the weapon is considered Holy/Unholy. May overcome standard weapon resistance of some creatures.
Duration: Events equal to half the Healer’s Tier,
Physical Representation: 100 word spell in spellbook, ritual magic roleplay. Blue ribbon and white ribbon tied on the weapon marked "RELIC"
Limitations: A player may only have one Relic in play at a time.
Prerequisite: Major Blessing
Spontaneous Spell: Healers use Spontaneous Spell to imbue an item, like a holy symbol, healing icon, or other focus item with a spell for later use. The player casts Spontaneous Spell first, and then the spell that they wish to be used instantly later, excluding the invocation line. When the player wants to unleash the stored spell they first must declare “Spontaneous Spell” and then recite the invocation line of the stored spell to be cast.
Physical Representation: 140 word spell in spellbook, holy symbol, healing icon, or other spiritual focus, and role play.
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per Healer Tier above 6. One spell at Healer Tier 6, plus one spell per Tier. All Spontaneous Spells reset after the conclusion of an event.

Healer’s Chant: (Combat Magic) While chanting this spell, and for every time the chant and roleplay is repeated within touch of the target, heal 1 wound or hit of damage to the desired target. If words can heal, they can also hurt. While chanting this spell, and for every time the chant and roleplay is repeated within touch of the target deal 1 wound or hit of damage to the desired target. Spell Lasts until interrupted, or chanting stops.
Physical Representation: 100 word spell in spellbook, and roleplay. The healing effect begins and The caster must create a six word chant that they will rhythmically speak or sing for the duration of the spell. For every Tier after 6th you may reduce the chant by one word.
Limitations: 1 use per event, with an additional use received every other tier. May only be used on players that are at maximum mortally wounded.

Necrotic Blast: (Combat Magic) The Mage is able to draw dark energy from the rotted roots of Leylines and cast a ball of necrotic energy at their foes. If the target is a player hit by the spell they are engulfed in a blast of necrotic energy, the life force is drained out of them and they are killed regardless of armor. If the target is an NPC it takes 4 wounds of Necrotic magic damage. Destroys shields on strike.
Damage: 4 points, Magic, Necrotic
Physical Representation: 80 word spell in spellbook and roleplay. 6” Black Spell Ball
Limitations: May be used 1x per day +1 per every 2 Mage Tiers.
Invocation Line: I [name] summon the powers of death to cast NECROTIC BLAST!

Bless/Curse Area: (Spell) The healer may now bless or curse an area rather than an object. The healer may use one ribbon equal to their healer tier in length (in feet) with which they can create an area of any shape. Anyone whose feet cross over the ribbon will be affected by the blessing or curse set onto the area.
Physical Representation: 140 words in spell book, red ribbon to outline area. If in combat, state loudly “CURSED AREA [EFFECT]” As the first person steps through (possible limit to curses with very obvious effect names, like curse of Slowness)
Limitations: May be used 1x per day, an additional use gained every other tier.
Prerequisite: Major Blessing, Major Curse

Cross-Training: At Tier 6 you may train in one of the other Skill Paths. The player must have the previous training level in the appropriate path to pursue the second stage of cross path training. Cross Training skills are repeatable.
Warrior Training 3: The Player dedicates time to learning the craft of warriors. They can choose one tier 3 or lower ability from the Healer skill path.
Mage Training 3: The Player studies the craft of the magi of the realm. They can choose one tier 3 or lower ability from the Mage skill path.
Rogue Training 3: The Player studies and watches the rogues around them. They can choose one tier 3 or lower ability from the Rogue skill path.
Tier 7 Abilities

Reject Death: The Healer can now imbue a single player (including themselves) with the power to rise after being mortally wounded once per event. The player, provided no “Coup de Grâce” finishing move is performed, may return to battle at a single hit point after 15 seconds of RP on the ground, wherein they call out to the healer, the healer’s patron, or another symbol to signify their return to play.
Physical Representation: 150 word spell performed on the player, with a charcoal or paint mark left on a visible area of the player denoting the symbol of the healer.

Create Sanctuary: A healer with this skill can now Create a Sanctuary for healing and giving aid to adventurers. Other healers, even without this skill, may assist in forming a Sanctuary. A sanctuary can encompass an area of 10 feet per healer tier that assists with creating the space. Inside the sanctuary, healer skills are not expended, and all skills are performed at twice the speed.
Physical Representation: 150 word Spell Read with other healers intending to create a sanctuary. Space must be clearly denoted with a Sanctified Location spell.
Prerequisites: Create/Destroy Holy Relic

Safeguard: (Spell) When cast, the recipient will be immune to the first instance of a blessing or curse they come into contact with. Subsequent or consistent contact with the same blessing or curse will still impart the effect.
Physical Representation: 140 words in spell book
Limitations: May be used once per day, +1 for every healer tier after 7.
Prerequisite: Master of the Tome
