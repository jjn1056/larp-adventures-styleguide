Cleaned from the Discord #in-game-rules channel export. Decorative images and Discord UI markup were removed; linked rule resources are preserved.

## Character Creation

The Character Creation process at Larp Adventures is flexible, allowing for all manner of concepts to come about.

You have up until you reach Tier 3, which is 7 attended events, to solidify your main class. At that point, your spent skill points become locked in and you cannot change your primary class.

[A handy How-To for Creation](01-character-creation-how-to.md)

## Professions

When you reach Tier 3, you are able to begin the path down a Profession. We currently have three available to every class. Professions cannot replace a main class, but can supplement for flavor and character development.

### Merchants

Shopkeeps of Osterra that choose to buy into the Merchant's Guild are afforded certain perks and protections. There is an application process, however, and you should seek out Trez Arrigo to apply.

Note: You may have a SHOP without being a part of the Merchant's Guild Game.

### Bardic Arcana

The Bardic Profession offers magic in small quantities to those with creative prospects.

Bardic skills and abilities can be found in [Bardic Arcana Skills & Abilities](06-bardic-arcana-skills-abilities.md).

### Artificers

Industrious and inventive Osterrans may find benefit in tinkering and learning how to craft or improve upon things already in play

Artificer skills and abilities can be found in [Artificer Skills & Abilities](07-artificer-skills-abilities.md).

### Scholar

Those more academically inclined may find the scholarly pursuits more to their taste. Lore builders take heed, there is much to learn still of Osterra and her workings.

Scholar skills and abilities can be found in [Scholar Skills & Abilities](08-scholar-skills-abilities.md).
